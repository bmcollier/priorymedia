Priory Records is Britain's Premier Church Music label, and has been in operation since 1980, promoting choral and organ music.

Thursday June 1st 2023 - Priory Launches First National Psalm Chant Competition

This Thursday the 1st of June, Priory are launching their first international Psalm Chant writing competition. The contest is open to anyone of any age, and aims to increase participation in church composition.
